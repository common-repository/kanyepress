„Keep squares out yo circle“ – Kayne West. Want quotes like this on your webpage? This plugin is what you need.

== Description ==

You ever desired to enrich your boring webpage with the philosophical, mind-opening, self-reflecting quotes by legend Kanye West? Well, this is your lucky day! KanyePress enables you to place randomly generated quotes of Kanye West wherever you wish. Here comes the trick: Copy the shortcode [kanyepress] and put it into the content editor of your page / post / whatever. Done. Made possible by https://kanye.rest/.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I implement it =

Simply paste the shortcode [kanyepress] into the content editor. This will be replaced by a randomly generated quote inside the frontend of your webpage.
